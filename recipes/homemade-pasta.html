Homemade Pasta Recipe

Family Favorites:Fresh Pasta

Making pasta from scratch is a rewarding experience that brings the family together. This recipe is simple and can be customized with various sauces.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. On a clean surface, make a mound with the flour and create a well in the center.
  2. Crack the eggs into the well, add salt and olive oil.
  3. Using a fork, beat the eggs gently, then gradually incorporate the flour until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Wrap in plastic wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll out the dough using a pasta machine or rolling pin to your desired thickness.
  7. Cut into your preferred pasta shape (fettuccine, tagliatelle, etc.).
  8. Cook in boiling salted water for 2-4 minutes until al dente.
  9. Serve with your favorite sauce and enjoy!

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use semolina flour or a mix of semolina and all-purpose flour.
  • Let the pasta dry for a few minutes before cooking to prevent sticking.
  • Experiment with adding herbs or spinach to the dough for different flavors and colors.

Serving Suggestions

Pair your homemade pasta with a simple tomato sauce, creamy Alfredo, or a fresh pesto. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs for an extra touch.

Storage

Fresh pasta can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days or frozen for up to 2 months. Make sure to dust with flour to prevent sticking before storing.

Nutrition Information

Per serving (based on 4 servings): Approximately 200 calories, 7g protein, 30g carbohydrates, 6g fat.

Conclusion

Homemade pasta is a delightful dish that can be enjoyed by the whole family. With just a few ingredients and some time, you can create a meal that is both satisfying and delicious. Enjoy your cooking!
