Lasagna Recipe

Family Favorites: Lasagna

This is a classic lasagna recipe that has been passed down through generations. It's a hearty and comforting dish that's perfect for family gatherings or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for about 5 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the mozzarella cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Add another layer of meat sauce, followed by another 4 noodles. Repeat with the remaining ricotta mixture and another layer of meat sauce.
  9. Top with the last 4 noodles, remaining meat sauce, and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna with a side salad or garlic bread!
