Couscous Recipe

Family Favorites: CousCous

This is our daughter's favorite recipe. Years ago, I pulled this recipe online, but have failed to find it since. This is a modified recipe from normal couscouse recipes that are available online. It is a great side dish for any meal, and can be made in advance and stored in the fridge for a few days. It is also a great way to use up leftover vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 8oz box of couscous
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup diced purple onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced olives (black or kalamata)
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper (any color)
  • 1/4 cup chopped Italian parsley
  • 1/4 cup diced radishes (3 large)
  • 1/2 cup seeded and diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halfed)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (8oz package)

Steps

  1. Cook CousCous over stovetop according to instructions listed on package.
  2. Place CousCous in freezer as you prepare remaining ingredients.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the diced onion, olives, bell pepper, parsley, radishes, cucumber, and cherry tomatoes. Feta cheese
  4. Add the cooled couscous to the vegetable mixture.
  5. In a small bowl, mix together remaining ingredients.
  6. Pour the dressing over the couscous salad and toss gently to combine.
  7. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
