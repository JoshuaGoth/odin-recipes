Baked Salmon Recipe

Family Favorites: Baked Salmon

This is a simple and delicious baked salmon recipe that has become a family favorite. It's easy to prepare and packed with flavor, making it perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  5. Brush the mixture over the salmon fillets.
  6. Top each fillet with lemon slices.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired and serve immediately.

Enjoy your delicious baked salmon with a side of vegetables or a fresh salad!
